Unfortunately, it can mean multiple things:

  1. The one most will think and automatically assume is that he likes you. Typically we think if a guy is looking at us and his lips are turned upward, he's interested.
  2. However, there are other reasons for grinning. One such horrible possibility is he's imagining hurting you. He could dislike you, or just likes violence in general, and is enjoying the image of you in pain. Or-»
  3. He may not be particularly interested in you romantically, but is interested in you physically. He's enjoying the idea of conquering you and making you another conquest. Or-»
  4. Maybe he finds you funny. If you don't think you're doing something particularly funny, then he may find something about your habits amusing.

There may be other reasons, but these are off the top of my head. Before you think I seem a bit obsessed with darkness in fellow humans, it's not that, it's your description. Grinning is odd. Smiling implies softness, no teeth. Grinning implies teeth. This changes things. When a person likes another, they usually smile. They're thinking soft things about you; their heart is involved.

Grinning while staring changes things. This is more aggressive, cocky. This is the image of a guy assured he can do or have what he wants, not having tender feelings.
