AS -
 Here are some basic uses:-
1. Adverb- used for comparisons
Eg: Her voice is sweet as honey.
2.Conjunction -to join 2 sentences(in the place of 'because')
Eg: She did not go to school as she was not feeling well.
3.Preposition
Eg: He was selected as the captain of the football team
HAS -
 It is the 3rd person singular present tense of 'have'
Eg: I have 3 dogs at home; You have 3 dogs at home;
She/he has 3 dogs at home.
It is also used in present participle
Eg: It has been a pleasure talking to you.
